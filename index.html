Our Program

We are developing a herd of Irish Dexter cattle with an emphasis on functionality, breed character, and long-term usefulness. Our goal is to produce cattle that fit smaller acreages, practical beef programs, and breeders who value sound, manageable stock.

Breeding Focus

Our breeding program emphasizes structural correctness, disposition, maternal traits, and cattle that work well in real-world conditions. We believe steady herd improvement matters more than chasing numbers or volume.

Herd Direction

C & S Cattle is built around careful herd development and honest representation. We are focused on producing useful Dexter cattle with consistency, longevity, and practical value for both beef and breeding programs.

Herd

Our herd is being developed with attention to foundation females, replacement quality, and herd sires that support the long-term goals of the program.

Foundation Cows

Females selected for disposition, maternal ability, sound structure, and consistency within the herd. These cows form the base of the program and influence its long-term direction.

Replacement Heifers

Young females representing the future of the herd. Selection is centered on growth, usability, breed character, and the kind of traits that build a dependable cow herd over time.

Herd Sires

Bulls chosen with an eye toward improving structure, temperament, and overall herd quality. The right herd sire plays a major role in consistency and long-term improvement.

Why Irish Dexter

Irish Dexter cattle are a strong fit for smaller acreages and practical cattle programs. Their size, versatility, and breed character continue to make them appealing to families, breeders, and owner-operators.

Manageable Size

Dexter cattle fit well on smaller farms and are often appreciated by producers looking for cattle that are easier to handle without losing usefulness.

Breed Character

The breed carries a distinct identity and heritage that appeals to those who value more than just scale. Dexter cattle offer both practicality and a strong sense of purpose.

Efficient Utility

For farms seeking useful beef cattle with flexibility and efficiency, Dexter cattle offer a practical fit and long-term value.
